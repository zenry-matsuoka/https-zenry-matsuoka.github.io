about
During the lockdowns caused by the COVID-19 outbreak, people were disconnected from one another, locked within their own houses with little interaction. In response to this, SoundBit, a project by placeholder, was created to create and share fun clips with friends.

As a team with little coding knowledge, there were bound to be struggles to complete the project by the deadline. However, through sheer luck, placeholder has produced a somewhat functioning website, allowing people to create bits of video and audio to share.

Enjoy!
